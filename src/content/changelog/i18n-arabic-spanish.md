QuenchWorks is now translated into Arabic and Spanish. Use the language switcher in the header to move between English, العربية, and Español; Arabic renders right-to-left. Every surface is localized: the image and chart catalog, the per-app pages, the roadmap, and all of the documentation, with links that keep you in your language. Translations are an initial pass and will keep improving.
